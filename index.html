About the Urbanome Project

UrbanomeFit is part of the broader Urbanome Project, an EU-funded initiative focused on creating healthier, more sustainable urban living environments.

The Urbanome Project works to build a comprehensive urban health model, combining environmental data, behavioural insights, and community wellbeing indicators to help cities become cleaner, greener, and more liveable.

UrbanomeFit translates this scientific foundation into a practical wellness app, empowering individuals to improve their daily habits while contributing anonymously to a better understanding of urban health.

Use of Google Services

UrbanomeFit uses Google Sign-In solely for secure authentication. Basic profile information (name and email) is used only to create and synchronize your UrbanomeFit account.

If you choose to connect Google Fit, UrbanomeFit may access activity-related metrics such as steps or heart rate in order to provide personalised insights and track your wellbeing progress.

The app does not sell, share, or use personal or health data for advertising. All data is handled in compliance with the Google API Services User Data Policy.
