UrbanomeFit

UrbanomeFit is a fitness and wellbeing companion app focused on promoting physical activity, mental wellness, and healthy daily habits. Features include: • Daily step tracking with progress indicators • Activity level analysis based on long-term step averages • Weekly missions that motivate users to stay active • Mood calendar to help track emotional wellbeing over time • Meditation and mindfulness tools • Environmental data, such as weather and air quality, used only to give activity recommendations • Lifestyle mini-games, such as the 3-30-300 rule game, to encourage greener and healthier behavior UrbanomeFit does not sell or share user data. All collected information is used solely to provide the app’s features and enhance the user's wellness experience.
