UrbanomeFit

UrbanomeFit is a fitness and wellbeing companion app designed to help users improve their daily activity levels, support mental wellness, and build healthier lifestyle habits. The app empowers users with insights, motivation, and tools to support physical and emotional wellbeing.

Main Features

  • Daily step tracking with progress indicators
  • Activity level analysis based on long-term step averages
  • Weekly missions designed to motivate users to stay active
  • Mood calendar for tracking emotional wellbeing over time
  • Meditation and mindfulness tools to support mental health
  • Environmental insights such as air quality and weather, used only to provide safe activity recommendations
  • Lifestyle mini-games (e.g., the 3-30-300 rule game) promoting greener and healthier behaviour

Why UrbanomeFit Requests User Data

UrbanomeFit uses Google Sign-In solely for secure authentication. Your email address is used to create your account and to synchronize your progress across devices.

The app does not access your emails, contacts, files, or any sensitive Google data. Environmental or location-based information is used exclusively to provide local activity and wellness recommendations.

UrbanomeFit does not sell, share, or use your personal data for advertising.
