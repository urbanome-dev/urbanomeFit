Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-08-18

This Privacy Policy applies to the UrbanomeFit app (“Application”) developed by UPCOM (“Service Provider”) and distributed as a free service. The service is provided on an “AS IS” basis.

Information Collected Automatically

The Application may automatically collect certain non-personal information when you use it, such as:

  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage analytics (e.g., pages viewed, session duration)

This information is used only for Application functionality, analytics, security, and performance improvement.

Location Data

The Application may collect your approximate location with your consent. This is used to:

  • Provide location-aware recommendations
  • Generate environmental context for features such as outdoor suggestions

Approximate location data may be transmitted securely to our backend server for the above purposes only. Precise GPS coordinates are not required and are not shared with third parties.

Health Connect Data Usage

The Application can access only your daily step count from Health Connect. This access is entirely optional and requires your explicit permission.

Requested Health Connect Permission:

  • Read – Step Count

Health Connect data is used solely to display and personalize your walking-related activity inside the Application. All processing of Health Connect data is performed locally on your device.

  • No Health Connect data is uploaded, stored, or transmitted off your device.
  • No Health Connect data is used for analytics, advertising, or marketing.
  • No Health Connect data is shared with any third parties.

You may revoke this permission at any time via:

Settings → Health Connect → App Permissions

The Application complies with the Health Connect by Android Permissions and Data Policy and the Limited Use Policy.

User-Provided Data

You may optionally provide personal information to enhance your experience, such as:

  • Weight or other well-being measurements
  • Wellness check-ins or preferences
  • Email address for account/login purposes

This data is not sourced from Health Connect. It is stored securely on servers operated by the Service Provider and used only to deliver in-app features such as progress tracking or personalized suggestions.

AI Chat Processing (OpenAI)

The Application includes a chat feature powered by the OpenAI API to offer wellness guidance and general support.

To generate a response, the following may be transmitted to OpenAI:

  • Your text messages sent in chat
  • Optional approximate location context (e.g., city) to recommend outdoor activities

Health Connect data is NOT shared with OpenAI.
Manually entered health data (like weight or mood) is NOT shared with OpenAI.
