Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-08-18

This privacy policy applies to the UrbanomeFit app (hereby referred to as the "Application") for mobile devices that was created by UPCOM (hereby referred to as the "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use “AS IS”.

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects certain information when you download and use it. This may include:

  • Your device’s Internet Protocol address (IP address)
  • The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and the time spent on those pages
  • Time spent using the Application
  • The operating system of your mobile device

Location Data

The Application collects your device’s approximate geographical location. This information is used in the following ways:

  • Geolocation Services: To provide personalized content, walking suggestions, and map-based functionality.
  • Analytics and Improvements: Aggregated and anonymized location data helps the Service Provider understand user behavior and improve the Application.
  • Backend Processing: Location data is securely transmitted to the Service Provider’s backend server in order to generate maps, routes, environmental information, and personalized recommendations.
  • Third-Party Services: Periodically, anonymized location data may be sent to external services to help enhance the Application and optimize performance.

Location data collected by the Application is never combined with Google Fit data.

Google Fit Activity Data

The Application connects to your Google Fit account using the Google Fitness API to read your daily walking activity, such as step count and walking distance.

Requested Scope:

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/fitness.activity.read

This allows the Application to read your activity data from Google Fit.

  • No Google Fit data is stored, uploaded, transmitted, or shared.
  • All Google Fit data is used temporarily and only displayed to you inside the Application.
  • Once the Application is closed, Google Fit data is not retained in any form.
  • We do not request or access any other Google Fit scopes (heart rate, body metrics, nutrition, etc.).
  • Google Fit data is never used for advertising, analytics, marketing, or any unrelated purpose.
  • Google Fit data is never sold or shared with third parties.

You may revoke the Application’s access to Google Fit at any time through your Google Account settings (Security → Third-party apps with account access).
